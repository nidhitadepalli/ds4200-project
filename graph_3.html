Analysis:

This geospatial map displays average purchase amounts across U.S. states, with darker colors representing higher spending. Alaska stands out with the highest average purchase values, while most states show moderate to lower spending. Each state tooltip provides detailed insights including average purchase amount, customer ratings, top product category, promo code usage rates, and subscription purchase percentages. While "Clothing" dominates as the preferred category in most states, New Mexico and West Virginia uniquely favor "Accessories" as their top category, suggesting distinct regional preferences or market conditions in these areas. These geographic spending patterns could reflect differences in economic conditions, regional consumer preferences, and the effectiveness of marketing strategies across different state markets.
